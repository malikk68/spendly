Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Data We Collect

To provide the Service, Spendly collects the information you give us directly: your name, email address, and password when you create an account, and the expense records you log — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions. We do not collect financial data from your bank or card accounts, since Spendly is a manual tracking tool.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to operate and improve Spendly: to authenticate your account, to display your expense history and summaries back to you, and to diagnose and fix technical issues. We do not use your data for advertising, and we do not sell your personal information to anyone.

3. Data Storage

Your account details and expense records are stored in Spendly's database and retained for as long as your account remains active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time, after which your records will be permanently removed within a reasonable period.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal or financial data with third parties for marketing purposes. We may rely on trusted infrastructure providers (such as hosting and email delivery services) to operate the Service, and those providers only process data as needed to deliver their service to us.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, or if you would like to request access to or deletion of your data, please reach out to us through your account settings or the support contact provided on this site.
